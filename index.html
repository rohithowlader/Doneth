Doneth v1.0.0

                                

                                    We felt to-do lists were broken - fundamentally because they were designed to list to-do's. In the end, they fell short because we kept adding and piling on to the list - often doing, and more often, not doing them.

                                    We also felt that seemingly simple, random, repititive tasks ended up taking time and headspace - so we built Doneth. Doneth is a multi-utility to-do list application that enables you to get things done.

                                    Powered by GPT-3, Doneth has a multitude of micro-apps that enable you to complete things in here itself. This is currently still in Beta - and you need a valid GPT-3 key of your own to use.
